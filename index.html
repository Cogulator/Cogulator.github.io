A simple human performance calculator developed by MITRE for building KLM, NGOMSL, and CPM-GOMS models.

Getting Started

If you’re ready to start modeling, getting Cogulator installed on a Mac or PC is fairly straight-forward.

1. Download and install Adobe AIR
2. Download and install the Cogulator Beta

The whole process shouldn’t take more than 2 or 3 minutes.

Alternatives

If Cogulator doesn't work for your purposes (say, you wanted to plug into a sim or build models graphically), consider CogTool, GLEAN, SANLab-CM, or Apex.


Open Source

Cogulator is provided gratis under an Apache 2.0 license. Feel free to look under the hood or pull the code. If you’d like to contribute to the project, get in touch. Cogulator is developed in Flash CS6 and deployed as an Air application.

Beta

This is beta software: don’t use Cogulator for critical work. Make sure your models folder (Documents/cogulator/models) is backed up regularly.
